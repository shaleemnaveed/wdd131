Limes are small, green citrus fruits. They belong to the same family as lemons. Lime 101 is your guide to start exploring these mouth-puckering fruits.

Limes have a long and flaourful history that stretches bak thousands of years. Originally believed to have grown in Southeast Asia, once carried on long voyages, from tropical islands to modern kitchens everywhere.

Limes aren't just tasty, they're full of surprises. Did you know limes glow under UV light or that they were once so valuable sailors were nicknamed "limeys"?
